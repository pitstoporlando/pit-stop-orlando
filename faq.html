Is Pit Stop car wash safe for my car?

We only use soft cloth materials that will not cause scratches or swirls. Pit Stop car wash only uses chemicals and processes that are safe for your vehicles’ finish and graphics.

Do you hand wash car and trucks?

Yes. If you are not sure if you car will fit into the car wash tunnel, proceed to the hand wash area and ask a staff member to check the clearance for you (7.2).

How long does an Express Hand Wash take?

15 minutes

How long does a Pro, Master Pro Hand Wash take?

25-30 minutes, depending on how busy the full service area is, but not longer than 30 minutes.

Can I leave my car for detailing?

Yes

Do you restore headlights and how long does it take?

Yes we restore headlights. About 30-40 minutes, depending on how cloudy the lenses are.
